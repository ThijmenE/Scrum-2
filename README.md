Controls:
Jumping = Spacebar
Walking = A & D
Double Jump = Jump while in air (you have 1 extra jump)
Wall Slide = walk towards and touch a wall
Wall Jump = Jump while wall sliding


How do i play the game?

Download and unzip the zip folder in the project.
Search for Scrum-2.exe and open it.
